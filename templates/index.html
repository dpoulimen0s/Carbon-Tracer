What we hope to achieve

Our aim is to create a sense of environmental responsibility for the daily actions of individuals.

As the quote above suggests, we want users to look at themselves and how they live their lives and focus on what they can do as just one person to make a difference.

What do we provide?

We aim to provide the means to both self-reflect on how we can improve ourselves as well as providing relevant information on what the average person can do to implement this change. After all you cannot expect people to change their behaviour for the better if they don’t know how their current actions are contributing to the issue nor if they don’t know what substitutes they can make to help the issue.

Importance of calculating our contribution

Your Actions Matter

In a world of 7 billion people and numerous high-polluting multinational companies, it can be difficult to find purpose in being kinder to the environment. It is easy to think that as just one small person in a large world that your actions as an individual dont make a difference, but you have to make the change you want to see in the world. Together if we all make the change to treat our environment with the care and respect it deserves then there is nothing that we can't achieve!

Why calculate your carbon footprint?

By taking the carbon footprint calculator you can identify the areas in your life style that impact our environment the most. Having the knowledge of what is adding to the problem is the first step. The next is to act on that knowledge.
